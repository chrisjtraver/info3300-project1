2014 Trends in NYC Marijuanna Arrests

Christopher Traver, Lylla Younes, Christopher Yoo

Map of Arrest Locations

This map displays all arrests for marijuana related offenses in NYC between January 1st, 2014 and December 31st, 2014. The street price of marijuana at the data of arrest is signified by the color of the data point. A scale describing the color gradient used to plot the points is detailed to the left of the map. The darker colored dots represent more expensive marijuana, while the lighter colored dots represent cheaper marijuana. The goal of this map is to illuminate different trends in the location and prices of marijuana for related arrests. An example of a trend that we noticed is that there are mostly arrests at higher price points in Queens compared to the other four boroughs.

Trends in Marijuana Price

This map displays all of the arrests for marijuana related offenses in NYC between January 1st, 2014 and December 31st, 2014. The street price of marijuana at the arrest data is signified by the color of the data point, which is also explained in the scale to the left of the map. As you can see, most of the marijuana related arrests occurred in clumps throughout the 5 boroughs.

Demographic Data of Arrestees

The graphs below display demographic data of marijuana related arrests in NYC. The graphs include breakdowns of the genders, races, and ages of offenders that were arrested for marijuana related charges.
